About Us

We are a team of six enthusiastic students from VIT Pune, united by our goal of bridging the gap in financial literacy among teenagers. Our goal is to provide simple, clear, and easy-to-use resources that can help young people make better financial decisions. With a user-friendly website and minimal features, we aim to make financial education accessible to everyone, so teens can confidently manage their money and plan for the future.
